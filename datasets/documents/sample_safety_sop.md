# SOP-12 Fall Response

## Scope

This policy applies when a fall, collapse, or loss-of-balance event is detected in the facility.

## Risk Rules

- Treat a suspected fall as `high` risk when continuous evidence exists for at least one subject.
- Do not mark an event `high` risk without evidence.

## First Actions

1. Call the on-site safety manager.
2. Dispatch the nearest responder to verify the subject condition.
3. Preserve the evidence clip and frame for review.

## Escalation

- Send an alert to the safety operations channel for high risk events.
